My Services

Dog Nose Work

Dog Nose Work: Scent Training Sport for Dogs. Nose work is a fun search and scenting activity for virtually all dogs and people. This easy to learn activity and sport builds confidence and focus in many dogs, and provides a safe way to keep dogs fit and healthy through mental and physical exercise.

Research

Every case is unique, and I take the time to understand the specific needs of each dog and owner. In all cases, I devote a lot of time to research dog's behavior and find the best solution for each case. My goal is not just to spend time with the dog, but to understand the causes of the problem in order to purposefully solve it.

Training

Dogs are smarter than we think. They can learn a lot of commands and tricks. I can teach your dog to sit, lie down, stay, come, heel, and more. I can also teach your dog to do some fun tricks like roll over, play dead, and more. This is a great way to bond with your dog and keep him mentally stimulated.
